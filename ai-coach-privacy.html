How Your Privacy is Protected

At AI Life Coach, we take your privacy seriously. Here's a detailed explanation of how we protect your data:

Local Storage

  • Browser-Based Storage: All your chat history and personal information (including your name) are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage.
  • No Server Storage: Your data never leaves your device except when you're actively chatting with the AI.
  • Data Location: Everything is stored locally on your device, giving you complete control over your data.

No External Tracking

  • No Cookies: We don't use cookies for tracking purposes.
  • No Analytics: We don't use any external analytics or tracking mechanisms.
  • Private Conversations: Your conversations are completely private to you.

User Control

  • Clear History: You can completely remove all stored data at any time using the "Clear History" button.
  • Export Data: Download your entire chat history using the "Export Chat" button.
  • Automatic Deletion: Your data is automatically removed when you:
    • Clear your browser history/data
    • Use private/incognito browsing
    • Manually clear the chat history

Chat API Security

  • Minimal Data Transfer: When you send a message, only that message and relevant context are sent to the AI service.
  • No Personal Identification: Personal identification is never included in API calls.
  • Secure Communication: All communication with the AI service is encrypted using HTTPS.

Data Persistence

  • Browser-Specific: Your chat history is specific to the browser you're using.
  • Device-Specific: Data doesn't sync between devices, ensuring local-only storage.
  • Session Independence: Using private/incognito mode ensures no data persists after closing the browser.
