Abstract

I am a Research Scientist at Uniswap Labs. My current research interests include empirical economics and protocol & mechanism design.

Prior to joining Uniswap Labs, I was a quantitative researcher at AQR Capital Management, with a focus on equity alpha signal research and portfolio risk modeling. Before that, I was a structured product and derivatives trader at KGS Alpha Capital Markets (now BMO Capital Markets).

I hold a Master's degree in Financial Mathematics from Princeton University and a dual degree in Mathematics and Economics from the University of Pennsylvania.
